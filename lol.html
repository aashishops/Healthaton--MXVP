About MXVP- EPISIGHT

MXVP- EPISIGHT

MPVX-EpiSight is an AI-driven disease prediction platform designed to enable early detection,
accurate diagnosis, and rapid responses to emerging epidemic threats. Utilizing deep learning
algorithms, MPVX-EpiSight analyzes and interprets diverse data inputs, leveraging a curated multi-
test dataset that includes monkeypox lesions and other rashes across various demographics.

ABOUT


MPVX-EpiSight impact is primarily in its ability to detect outbreaks early, which enables timely testing, contact tracing, and containment
measures. This proactive approach helps mitigate the epidemic's effects, strengthens global health security, and provides rapid, accurate
identification of potential monkeypox cases. As a result, professionals can make informed decisions and implement effective interventions,
ultimately improving epidemic management and control.
